Sun of Blood and Ruin by Mariely Lares is set in the sixteenth-century New Spain where a new legend begins after rumors of a new vigilante serving justice spread. 
Sun of Blood and Ruin is a reimagining of Zorro, but this time features a heroic sorceress.
Leonora de Las Casas Tlazohtzin is a respectable law-abiding lady who never leaves her castle and is promised to the heir of the Spanish throne, while Pantera hidden behind a uses her magic and legendary swordplay to fight the tyranny of the Spanish rule. Nobody suspects that these two could be the same person.
