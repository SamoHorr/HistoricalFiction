Washington Black by Esi Edugyan is a historical fiction that tells a story of friendship and betrayals.
The story follows an eleven-year-old field slave , Washington Black or Whash,  who hasn't known any other life than being a slave on a Barbados sugar plantation where he was born.
Esi is terrified of the possibility of abuse and cruelty when his master's eccentric brother, Christopher Wilde, also nicknamed as Titch, chooses him to be his manservant. 
In the book Washington Black, we follow the adventures of both these characters as they travel through the eastern coast of America to the Arctic.
