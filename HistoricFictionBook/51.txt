The Paris Wife by Paula McLain is a tale of a whirlwind love, betrayal, and romance. The novel narrates the love affair between Ernest Hemingway and his first wife, Hadley Richardson and is told from Richardson’s perspective. We follow the two after their engagement in Chicago as they move to Paris in the 1920s. And how as the Jazz Age starts to make a buzz causing Ernest to throw himself into his work how their relationship strains as it goes through many ups and downs. We get to follow the five year marriage between McClains’ Hadley and the famed novelist.
