The Other Boleyn Girl is a historic romantic drama reimagining the events surrounding the life of the historic Anne Boleyn, one of the many wives of King Henry VIII. It is a fictionalized account of the lives of the 16th century. This time the tale tells the story of two sisters competing for the greatest prize: The love of a king. “You have to keep him coming forward, but never let him think that you come forward yourself” This quote from the book showcases the ambitious plots of the Boleyn family to use the girls to improve their social standing.
