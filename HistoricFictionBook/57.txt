“An utterly original, wild ride … that turns the traditional redemption narrative on its head.” -XOCHITL GONZALEZ. New York Times bestselling author of Olga Dies Dreaming said about Elizabeth Gonzalez James' novel named ‘The Bullet Swallower’. A dazzling western mixed with magical realism, the bullet Swallower follows the story of a Mexican bandido in 1895 as he went off for Texas to save his family only to meet a mysterious figure who came to collect a cosmic debt in  generations in the making. The main character receives the nickname the Bullet Swallower after he survives a shoot-out and swears revenge.
