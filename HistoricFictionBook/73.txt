The plot of the novel of 'The Sound of the Mountain' by 'Yasunari Kawabata talks about Shingo Ogata, a 62 year old businessman living in Kamarkura and working in Tokyo who's close to retirement. Shingo experiences temporary lapses in memory and recalls strange and disturbing dreams upon waking up after hearing titular noises "like wind, far away, but with a depth like a rumbling of the earth." Shingo takes these strange experiences and especially the sounds as an omen of his impending death, which causes him to re-evaluate his life, he observes and questions his relations with his family members.
