Alex and Eliza author Melissa De La Cruz is a true Hamilton fan, inspired by the Tony Award-Winning Hamilton musical created by Lin-Manuel, Melissa wrote Alex and Eliza. This novel is a romantic historical fiction set in Albany, New York in 1777 during the American Revolution. A love story that changed history between Alexander Hamilton a young colonel and Eliza one of the three daughters of the Schuyler’s, who were one of the country’s founding families. “Their romance shaped a nation. The rest was history.” This phrase from the summary of the book best explains the essence of the book.
