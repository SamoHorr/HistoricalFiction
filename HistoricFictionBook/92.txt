To Woo and to Wed by Martha Waters is the fifth and final installment of the Regency Vows series that follows the heir to a dukedom, once very much in love, as they reunite years later to fake an engagement for the benefit of her sister.
West and Sophie hatch a plan to fake their engagement as her sister refuses to move forward with her engagement until her sister settles down.
“To Woo and to Wed is a perfect ending to the Regency Vows series, solidifying its status as one of the most entertaining historical romance series on shelves today.”
