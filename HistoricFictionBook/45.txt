Property by acclaimed author Mary Reilly brings us a different point of view on the horrors of slavery. As this book gives us the perspective of a rarely heard voice: the voice of a woman slave holder. Manon Gaudet a self-absorbed, petulant, and bored woman, who comes to a Louisiana sugar plantation in 1828 to become the owner’s bride. She becomes obsessed with her slave Sarah, who has a bitter relationship with Manon’s husband. And as the whisper of rebellion starts to spread, we see the events unfold as we learn about Manon’s past and present, her longings and dreams.
