Signal Fires by Dani Shapiro a novel that begins with a horrible ending. Set in 1985 the story begins with three intoxicated teenagers that go for a car ride that proves to be fatal. The details of the accident were kept under wraps and kept as secret, a secret that will haunt one family forever. Then decades later we follow the doctor who was at the scene of the accident, and he befriends his eleven-year-old neighbor right near the spot where the accident happened. Shapiro manages to capture how life is just made up of random moments and missed opportunities.
