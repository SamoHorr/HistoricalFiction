“I am now a condemned traitor . . . I am to die when I have hardly begun to live.” Innocent Traitor by author Alison Weir is a novel that blends the historical accurate facts with a bit of fiction to give us a new insight on one of the most turbulent and unstable times in the English Monarchy. It is the story of Jane Grey nicknamed “the Nine Day’s Grey” who is a fifteen-year-old girl who unknowingly finds herself at the center of the biggest religious and civil unrest that almost managed to topple the fabled House of Tudor.
