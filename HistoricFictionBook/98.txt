In The Warm Hands of Ghost, a hauntingly beautiful novel where we follow a combat nurse that searches for her presumed to-be dead brother during the Great War.
January 1918. Laura is a revered field nurse until she was wounded and discharged from the medical corps, she returns home leaving her brother Freddie behind. After she receives the word of Freddie’s death in combat, she feels something isn’t right and returns to Belgium to uncover the truth.
November 1917. Freddie awakens trapped in an overturned pillbox with a wounded enemy soldier, they escape only to fall prey to something else.
