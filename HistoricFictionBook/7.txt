Les miserable is a French historical novel set in impoverished nineteenth century Paris. It consists of several interrelated stories following the lives of different characters but mainly focuses on Jean Valjean an escaped convict that tries to put his criminal past behind and try a life as a respected member of the community but is at risk by the Inspector Javert. Les miserable is a tale of social injustice that shines a light on how deprivation can lead to crime. Which can be highlighted by this quote ” If the soul is left in darkness sins will be committed ”.
