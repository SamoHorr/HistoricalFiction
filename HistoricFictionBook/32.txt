“Oliver Twist with a twist…Waters spins an absorbing tale that withholds as much as it discloses. A pulsating story.”—The New York Times Book Review of Fingersmith by Sarah Waters. A historical thriller fiction book that tells the story of Sue Trinder, an orphan raised by Mrs. Sucksby as her own. One day Sue meets a gentleman, a con man that engages Sue services to help him seduce a naïve gentlewoman named Maud by agreeing to share Maud’s vast inheritance.
 In hopes of paying her adoptive family back Sue agrees to the plan but soon begins to pity her mark.
