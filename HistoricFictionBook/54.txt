Published in 2022 by the bestselling author of The Aviator’s Wife, The Children’s Blizzard By Melanie Benjamin is a story based on the true story of the Schoolhouse Blizzard of 1988. A fictionalized take on the gripping story of survival and resilience. “The two prepared to head out into the storm that was not letting up, thank God; maybe this thing was going to turn out to be a tragedy, after all!” as we follow to schoolteacher sisters, Raina and Gerda Olsen, as they are faced with the difficult task of how to save their students from an unexpected blizzard.
