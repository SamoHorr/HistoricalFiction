Beneath a Scarlet Sky a novel by Mark T. Sullivan is based on the true story of a forgotten hero, a tale of one man’s incredible courage through one of history’s darkest hours.
It follows the journey of Pino, a normal Italian teenager that was forced to join the army as a German soldier, but after an injury sideline him he becomes the driver of Adolf Hitler’s left hand in Italy, allowing him to spy for the Allies.
“Maybe that’s all it takes for the future to exist, Pino thought. You must imagine it first. You must dream it first.”
