A Tempest of Tea by Hafsah Faizal is the first book of the Blood and Tea series a historical fiction novel mixed with a little bit of paranormal.
The story follows an orphan girl and her crew who get tangled in a heist with vampires. Arthie Casimie is a criminal mastermind and collector of secrets, with a prestigious tearoom by day and an illegal bloodhouse catering to the vampires by dark. But when her establishment is threatened, Arthie is forced to strike a deal with an alluring adversary.
“Dark, action-packed, and swoon worthy, this is Hafsah Faizal better than ever.”
