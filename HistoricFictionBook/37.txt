The Sympathizer by Viet Thanh Nguyen, the winner of the 2016 Pulitzer Prize for Fiction as well as seven other awards is the novel of the year. It is an epic story of love and betrayal set in the 1970s that narrates about a communist double agent a half-French, half-Vietnamese army captain who comes to America after the fall of Saigon to build a new life with other Vietnamese refugees in Los Angeles while also simultaneously spying and reporting back to his communist superiors in Vietnam.
It is a gripping espionage novel with a powerful message about love and friendship.
