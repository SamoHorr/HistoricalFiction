Lonesome Dove by Larry McMurty is the first published book of the Lonesome Dove series but the third one in the series chronologically. The book also won the 1986 Pulitzer Prize for Fiction and was adapted as a mini-series. The novel is set during the Old West and centers around the relationship between several retired Texas Rangers and their adventures as they drive a cattle herd from Texas to Montana. “It ain’t dying I’m talking about, it’s living. I doubt it matters where you die, but it matters where you live.” ~spoken by Augustus McCrae” ― Larry McMurtry, Lonesome Dove.
