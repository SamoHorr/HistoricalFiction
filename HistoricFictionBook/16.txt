“All for one and one for all.” As Alexandre Dumas famously wrote in his French historical adventure novel that is famous worldwide. The Three Musketeers is set in 1625 Paris follows Artagnan journey to follow his dream of becoming a musketeer and a guard to the king Louis XIII. We follow Artagnan as he and his three companions navigate the court and the intrigue and treason that surrounds it. Written with the collaboration of ghostwriter Auguste Maquet as was most of Dumas work this book contains heroism, comradery and chivalrous swordsmen who are always working on the side of justice.
