The English Patient is a 1992 novel by Michael Ondaatje. The book follows four different people from four different cultures all brought together under one roof in an Italian villa during the Italian Campaign of the Second World War. A heavily injured and unrecognizably burned presumably English man, a Canadian army nurse, a British combat engineer of Indian origin, and an Italian-Canadian spy. During their time together the four share their traumatic experiences of the war and their mutual desire to survive. The English Patient has a unique way of talking about a global conflict while showcasing the different cultures.
