Wolf hall by Hilary Mantel, the first installment of Mantel’s trilogy, lets us see the events that unfolded during Tudor England with King Henry VIII, Catherine of Aragon, Anne Boleyn through the eyes of the King’s trusted lawyer Thomas Cromwell. Thomas played a vital role in helping Henry break away from the Catholic Church to divorce Catherine and marry his new wife, Anne. It later helped him ascend to the top and rise to power. It is a well-researched and well written book that allows readers to immerse themselves in England court during the 1500s. in its most scandalous years.
