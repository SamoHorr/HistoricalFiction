The unsettled by Ayana Mathis is a multi-generational novel set in the 1980s in a politically and racially turbulent Philadelphia and also in the tiny town of Bonaparte in Alabama, The main plot of the novel is about a mother , Ava Carson fighting for her and her ten year old son survival. 
Ava is determined to rescue and protect her son from the perils of the family shelter they are in and also from the troubled past that landed them there.
“When you’re somebody’s mother you can’t admit that you can’t manage the mothering.”
― Ayana Mathis, The Unsettled
