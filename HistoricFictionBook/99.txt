War Trash is a novel written by the Chinese author Ha Jin, it takes the form of a memoir written by the fictional character Yu Yuan. Yu Yuan becomes a soldier in the Chinese People’s Volunteer Army and is sent to Korea to fight on the Communist side in the Korean War.
Most of the memory describes Yu Yuan's experience of that time, especially after he was captured by United Nations forces and imprisoned as a POW.
“To witness is to make the truth known, but we must remember that most victims have no voice of their own.” – War Trash.
