Where the Crawdads sing By Delia Owens, set in the later 1696 in a quiet town on the North Carolina coast. When the handsome Chase Andrews is found dead, the local suspect Kya Clark rumored as the “Marsh Girl” that has haunted Barkley Cove. But Kya isn’t as the locals dubbed her, she is a sensitive and intelligent girl, who survived on her own for years finding friends in gulls and lessons in the sand. When two young men from town become intrigued by her wild beauty, Kya opens herself to a possibility of a new life-until the unthinkable happens.
