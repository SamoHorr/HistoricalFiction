The Second Life of Mirielle West by Amanda Skenandore set in 1920s Louisiana is a story based on the true story of America’s only leper colony. As we follow Amanda Skenandore a silent film star’s wife as her life is turned upside down when she’s sent to Carville Lepers Home in Louisiana after a doctor suspects her of having the incurable disease. Stuck in a prison disguised as a care center, Mirielle must redefine her purpose and make a life that is worth fighting for.  How far can one socialite fall? “Don’t you want to be alive before you die?”
