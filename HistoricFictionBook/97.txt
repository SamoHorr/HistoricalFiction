The Hidden Life of Cecily Larson by Ellen Baker is set in 1924, as we follow Cecily Larson at age four as she gets reluctantly dropped off at an orphanage in Chicago by her mother till she's able to afford to support both of them.
But her mother never returns and at age seven Cecily is sold into a traveling circus to perform as the “little sister” to the bareback rider Isabelle DuMonde. She finds a family in that circus and Isabelle.
In 2015, Cecily is 94 and after she was surprised with an at-home DNA test, secrets are revealed.
