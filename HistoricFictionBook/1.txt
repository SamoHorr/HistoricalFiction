Maude Horton’s Glorious Revenge by Lizzie Pook is a book full of twists and turns that incorporates both the historical and mystery thriller genres in a suspenseful and addictive story of a young woman named Maude searching for her missing sister with the help of clues in her diary. Set in London’s seedy underbelly during the Victorian murder mania era, Maude uncovers dark secrets and meets a mix of interesting but sinister characters.
For any reader that enjoys a good historical fiction with a bit of mystery, Maude Horton’s Glorious Revenge is a must add to the 2024 reading list.
