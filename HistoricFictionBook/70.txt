Let Us Descend by Jesmyn Ward was nominated for Best Historical Fiction of 2023.
“‘Let us descend,’ the poet now began, ‘and enter this blind world.’” — Inferno, Dante Alighieri. Let Us Descend is a reimagining of American slavery. It’s a heart-wrenching, searching and harrowing novel with transcendent love. The novel’s journey begins from the rice fields of the Carolinas to the slave markets of New Orleans and into the fearsome heart of a Louisiana sugar plantation. “There is life in the sinking, the water at my feet says. Life in the descent.” – Page 260, Let Us Descent by Jesmyn Ward.
