The Ghost Bride, the literary debut book of the author Yangsze Choo with a refreshingly original voice, is a breathtaking cross genre historical novel that broaches on several topics such as colonialism, romantic intrigue, adventure, coming of age and much more. This novel takes you through a speculative walk-through life and death infused with interesting Chinese folklore with fascinating dreamlike twists. “It seems to me that in this confluence of cultures we had acquired one another’s superstitions without necessarily any of their comforts.” –Yangsze Choo, The Ghost Bride. This book also helps readers learn about old Malaysia culture and history.
