One of the many works of the famed William Shakespeare, Hamlet is a tragedy divided in five acts written sometime between 1599-1601, the play talks about Prince Hamlet and his attempt of revenge on his uncle who murdered his father for the throne.
Shakespeare’s telling of the story of Prince Hamlet, the prince of Denmark was created from different various sources most notably from Books III and IV of Saxo Grammaticus’s 12th-century Gesta Danorum. And from Shakespeare Hamlet spoken by Hamlet one of the most famous quotes known by man “To be or not to be, that is the question.”
