A Gentleman in Moscow by Amor Towles is a story about a revolutionary era that defined international history for decades to come. A historical novel highly recommended by Bill Gates. The novel follows Count Alexander Illyich Rostov who although his good-natured personality his birth status makes him a target in the new regime installed by the Bolshevik Revolution in 1922. The Count moves to the luxury Metropol hotel across the Kremlin watches as his life slowly crumbles under the pressure of the revolution. A New York Times bestseller novel that tells a classic story of a man searching for meaning.
