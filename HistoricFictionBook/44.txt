A ballad of Love and Glory by Reyna Grande, set during the American War, this novel explores different themes such as colonialism, war and the star-crossed love of our two main characters. Ximena Salome, an army nurse, and John Riley, an Irish immigrant who deserts the Yankee army to fight on the Mexican side as they get swept up into a passionate romance as the dangers of war intensifies around them.
Praised for its historical accuracy and for shining a light on an overlooked period of history, this enemies-to-lovers book is a must read with its compelling and complex characters.
