Sea of Poppies by the Indian author Amitav Ghosh is the first installment of the series the 'Ibis trilogy', and it was even shortlisted for the Man Booker Prize in 2008. Ghosh uses this trilogy to voice out her disagreement on imperialism and colonialism "the Ibis trilogy is Ghosh's most vehement indictment of the source of imperialism and colonialism.".
We follow our main characters: Deeti, an ordinary village woman , an American sailor named Zachary Reid , And Benjamin Burnham , an evangelist opium trader. The story begins before the First Opium War on the banks of the holy Ganges.
