First installment of the ‘Woman of Troy’ ‘The Silence of the Girls’ by Pat Barker we see the Trojan War through the eyes of Briseis a queen of one of Troy’s neighboring kingdoms, she becomes one of the many conquered women of the Greek army after Achilles sacked her city and murdered her husband and brothers. “The defeated go down in history and disappear, and their stories die with them.”- Pat Barker, Briseis is one of the many thousands of women who faced horrific circumstances as an aftermath of the war and whose voice was lost and erased by history.
