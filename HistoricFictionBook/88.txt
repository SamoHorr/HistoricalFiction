Daisy Jones & The Six by Taylor Jenkins Reid winner for the Best Historical Fiction in 2019 narrates the story of Daisy. Daisy is a girl coming of age in L.A. in the late sixties, whose voice gets noticed as well as her beauty, a heedless beauty that makes people do crazy things. Also getting noticed is The Six, a band led by the brooding Billy Dunne.
A producer soon realizes the key to immediate success is putting Daisy and Bill together.
“It’s like some of us are chasing after our nightmares the way other people chase dreams.”- Taylor Reid.
